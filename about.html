Our Genesis

Born from a vision to bridge Africa's sports development gap, Ni-Wakati Sports Africa emerged as a response to the untapped potential within communities across the continent. "Ni-Wakati" ‚Äî Swahili for "It's Time" ‚Äî reflects our urgent call to action.

We observed a disconnect: while Africa produces world-class athletic talent, grassroots sports infrastructure remains underdeveloped. Our founders ‚Äî sports professionals, community organizers, and development experts ‚Äî united around a singular mission: to build inclusive sports ecosystems that serve as catalysts for social transformation.

What began as local community soccer programs in Nairobi's informal settlements has evolved into a pan-African movement. Today, we partner with governments, international brands, and local communities to create sustainable sports development models that prioritize accessibility, innovation, and measurable impact.
